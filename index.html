Features

Make your annoying tasks even less annoying! You can now easily code very powerful scripts through the help of qRunner

User Focused
Through many accessibility features and the open nature of qRunner, you will be able to easily automate a lot of things, you could not automate previously
Shortcuts
Invoke your scripts easily through the use of keyboard shortcuts. This gives you new and extremely powerful tools right at your fingertips
Built-in Typescript Support
This gives you a powerful typesystem to easily develop your own tools without bugs. With the strongly typed API, you can easily do anything imaginable
Easy user input
Obtain user input easily through APIs like arg(...) or textarea(...). No hard-to-read, annoying and complicated --help flags anymore!
Easy sharing
Share your scripts easily right from your prompt by publishing them to Github gists, which allows other users to easily import them right from their prompt!
Scripts work from the terminal
Your scripts can also be launched from the terminal, and thus can be bound to a streamdeck. Supply arguments, which will be picked before prompting the user to input by the arg(...) function
